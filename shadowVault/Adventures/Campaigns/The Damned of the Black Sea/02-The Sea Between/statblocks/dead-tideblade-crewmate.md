# Dead Tideblade Crewmate

A drowned shipmate the party knew alive — waterlogged, calm, terribly familiar. They board to talk, not to fight; forced to it, they fight reluctantly and defensively, trying to hold and take rather than harm. Destroyed, they sigh like men relieved of watch.

> [!info]+ Dead Tideblade Crewmate — LV 3
> **AC** 12 · **HP** 14 · **MV** near (swim) · **AL** C
>
> **ATK** 1 boarding axe +3 (1d8) or 1 grasp +3 (1d4)
>
> | STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
> |:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
> | +1  | +0  | +1  | +0  | +1  | +1  |
>
> **A Face You Know.** The first time it attacks, each PC who knew them in life makes a DC 12 WIS check or takes −1 on attacks against it for the rest of the fight (grief).
>
> **Cold Grip.** On a grasp hit, DC 12 STR check or the target is held and pulled close-distance toward the rail; DC 12 STR as an action to break free.
>
> **"It doesn't hurt, after."** Once per fight, it speaks instead of attacking: one PC of its choice makes a DC 12 WIS check or loses their action this round, listening.
