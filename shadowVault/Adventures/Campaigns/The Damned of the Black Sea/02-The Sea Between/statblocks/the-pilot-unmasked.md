# The Pilot (Unmasked)

When the offer fails, the neat dry man stops pretending to be small. He is still polite — the gaff he fights with is handled like a clerk's pen, and he collects the fallen the way paperwork collects everything, eventually. He does not flee; if truly beaten, he lifts his hat, steps backward off the rail, and is not in the water when it closes. See [[the-pilot]] for the man before the mask comes off.

> **The Offer Stands (1/day).** Instead of attacking, he re-extends the bargain to one PC who is below half HP or entangled: DC 15 WIS check or the PC loses their action this round, listening — and takes −1 on attacks against the Pilot until they refuse him out loud.
>
> **Collected, Not Killed.** A PC the Pilot drops to 0 HP is not dying — they are being carried, gently, toward his boat by the nearest boatman. Anyone may interrupt the carry (free the body, block the rail); a PC who reaches his boat is gone, final.
>
> **Never in the Light.** He cannot enter the ferryman's lantern-light; attacks made from inside it against him are at advantage.
