# The Pirate Captain's Logbook

*Player handout — found in the derelict pirate camp, clutched in a dead pirate's hands.*

---

**Day 1 on the island**

At last, land under our feet. An island on none of our charts, but full of promise. The jungle is dense as a rampart, the air heavy as a wet coat. The men curse the heat, but I feel… something. Something calling us.

The natives are said to live in the east, a small tribe. Savages, the men say. But savages know their homeland. And this island has secrets I will unravel — with or without their help.

We will make camp first, before we seek the village.

**Day 5 on the island**

We have found the village. A wretched heap of huts, but at its center stands a shrine older than anything human hands could build. The natives kept us away from the shrine. I want to know what they are hiding from us.

**Day 6 on the island**

The natives would not help us. They spoke in their rasping tongue, warned us with wild gestures. They said the "Warden" would devour us if we went further. Nonsense. We need answers, and they have them. Tomorrow at dawn we storm their village. I tolerate no obstacles.

**Day 7 on the island**

The village has fallen. It was no battle — more a massacre. They fled into the jungle, screaming like animals.

We examined the shrine. It shows a sequence of images: a shard, restless. A basin that breaks. And a pearl that takes its place.

I do not fully understand it yet. But I will.

**Day 9 on the island**

We have discovered the lagoon, north of our camp. A place so still that even the waves fall silent. Beneath the surface swim fish made of light — ghost fish, the men say. I saw them myself: transparent, shimmering, as if formed from moonlight.

Something glows on the bottom. A soft, bluish light pulsing in the sand. I believe they are pearls. Perhaps *the* pearl the shrine means. Perhaps the key.

**Day 10 on the island**

Two men have not returned. The others claim cold hands pulled them under. Nonsense. They drowned because they were weak.

But the pearl they brought… It is warm. Alive. When I hold it, I feel a soft throbbing, as if it were speaking with my heartbeat. I believe it is important. I believe it is necessary.

**Day 11 on the island**

We have found tracks. Claws, torn deep into the rock. A smell like wet death hangs in the air. The men want to go back to the ship. They tremble. They pray.

But I feel stronger. The island speaks to me. It wants me to go on.

**Day 12 on the island**

We have reached the skull temple. It lies in the north, exactly as the shrine showed. A rock shaped like a skull, with an entrance like a yawning maw. A bluish light pulses deep within. It calls me.

The men refuse to go further. Cowards. Tomorrow I descend alone. With the pearl. With courage. With destiny.

**Day 13 on the island**

Something moves in the temple. Something old. Something that guards the shard. I hear it breathing. I hear it *thinking*.

I will take the shard. It belongs to me. To me…

…and to the deep.
