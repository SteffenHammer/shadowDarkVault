# The Shard of the Deep

A sharp-edged fragment of blue light that writhes like liquid energy, slowly turning on its own axis above a stone basin. Its pulsing glow throws long, distorted shadows, and it brightens when someone approaches — as if it *recognizes* them.

- **Type:** wondrous (campaign artifact)
- **Effect:** The Shard has a will of its own. It whispers to those near it and is the key to the ritual described in the [[ritual-book]]. It can only be taken safely from its basin if a [[lagoon-pearl|lagoon pearl]] is set in its place; otherwise the Warden of the Deep wakes and will not let it leave the chamber.
- **Curse / Cost:** Whoever carries it begins to change — dreams of the deep, voices, and the slow attention of the Black Sea itself.
- **History (GM only):** A splinter of the bound Leviathan, the god-beast sealed beneath the sea in the temple's oldest days. The shrine reliefs on the island record the covenant: the Shard rests in the basin; if it is lifted, the basin must not be left empty, or the seal begins to fail. Rauthorn needs it to perform the ritual he believes grants immortality.
- **Found in:** the Chamber of the Shard — see [[04-the-shard|Act 4: The Shard]].
