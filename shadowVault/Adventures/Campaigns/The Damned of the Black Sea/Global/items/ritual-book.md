# The Ritual Book

An old, mystical tome that [[captain-rauthorn|Captain Rauthorn]] pulled from blood and smoke aboard a butchered navy warship. Since that day, he has not been the same.

- **Type:** wondrous (campaign artifact)
- **Effect:** Describes an ancient ritual of the Black Sea whose key is the [[shard-of-the-deep|Shard of the Deep]]. The book whispers to its reader; Rauthorn follows little else now.
- **Curse / Cost:** Its reader withdraws, sleeps less, and begins to hear the deep. Rauthorn's pallor, black-blue veins, and flickering blue eyes date from the day he opened it.
- **History (GM only):** Where the navy ship carried it from, and who wrote it, the source leaves untold — the book's promise of immortality is a lie: the ritual makes its performer unhuman and feeds the Leviathan's awakening.
- **Found in:** Rauthorn keeps it. It is first seen in [[01-prologue-the-tideblade|the prologue]].
